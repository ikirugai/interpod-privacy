InterPod may use third-party services to provide its features, including audio processing, content delivery, and cloud storage. These services are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

The app may use platform-provided cloud services (such as iCloud or Google Backup) to sync or back up your app data. This data is tied to your platform account and is not accessible to us.

Your Data

You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Children

InterPod is not directed at children under 13.

Changes
