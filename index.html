InterPod - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 April 2026

InterPod is a podcast player with live transcription, developed by ikirugai. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your data.

Data Collection

InterPod does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use. Your podcast library, listened history, and playback positions are stored locally on your device.

Audio Transcription

When you play a podcast episode, InterPod may send audio segments to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. This is done through our secure proxy server and only occurs for episodes that do not already have a transcript provided by the podcast publisher. The audio segments are processed by OpenAI in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not store your audio data on our servers.

Device Verification

InterPod uses Apple App Attest (iOS) and Google Play Integrity (Android) to verify that API requests come from a genuine app installation. This verification involves exchanging cryptographic tokens with Apple or Google's servers. No personal information is collected through this process.

Cloud Sync (iOS)

On iOS, your podcast library and listened states are synced using Apple's CloudKit service, tied to your iCloud account. This sync happens automatically and allows you to restore your library if you reinstall the app or switch devices. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Cloud Backup (Android)

On Android, your app data is included in Android Auto Backup, which is managed by Google and tied to your Google account. This backup happens automatically and is governed by Google's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

InterPod uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI Whisper API — for audio-to-text transcription of podcast episodes
  • Apple CloudKit (iOS) — for syncing your library across devices
  • Apple iTunes Search API — for discovering and searching podcasts

InterPod does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All podcast data (subscriptions, episodes, playback positions, transcripts) is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database. Downloaded audio files are stored in the app's local storage. No personal data is stored on our servers.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud/Google account, you have full control over your data at all times. You can delete all app data by deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

InterPod is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@ikirugai.com.
